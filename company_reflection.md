**Why do you think Focus Bear was created?**
FOcus Bear was created to combat the difficulties of ADHD and ASD students with navigating around their educational life, in high school and univeristy, and make sure that they can cope with their studies.

**What problem is Focus Bear solving?**
Focus Bear is trying to solve the hyperactivity of ADHD students and the problem of ASD students when it comes to change and the overstimulation of new environments that may overwhelm them to the point where they aren't able to focus on their work.

**Why do you think this mission is important?**
This mission is important because there is an increasing population of individuals who are being diagnosed with ADHD or ASD, or are displaying ADHD/ASD symptoms, meaning that they are receiving unequal treatment when it comes to their studies.
